EXCLUSIVE SNEAK PEAK of May's Playable-Character Sheets!

Welcome, traveller... to the DEFINITELY AND ABSOLUTELY EXCLUSIVE SNEAK PEAK... of my very own... CHARACTER SHEETS!

I mean, that's what you came for, isn't it? So that's what you'll get, I suppose!

So - without further ado! Let's get to the character sheets!

DISCLAIMER: I am only including the sheets I've made for the characters that actually appear for several sessions of a campaign; throwaway characters, character stat blocks made for past campaigns with my IRL friends, and characters made for discontinued-before-it-even-started campaigns will not be included.


CALYPSO FALL

VIEW ON DNDBEYOND | VIEW PDF OF CHARACTER SHEET

This character over here is Calypso Fall, the very first character of mine who got accepted into a campaign! (A.K.A. the very first campaign I've ever participated in, even if for a short period of time.) He's a wood elf paladin, and I played him for the very few sessions of the campaign a few friends were gracious enough to let me join in at the very last minute.

Playing him, even for such a short time, was just so much fun, even if I was a complete beginner then! I might only be saying that because it was my first game ever, but still! Point still stands! I gave him a light Irish accent on a whim - an accent that I'm not at all familiar with actually performing, hah - so I had many, many good laughs whilst playing. This character, Calypso, will forever be remembered in my poor heart, for reasons that I hope are obvious.


LORIELL HAWTHORN

VIEW ON DNDBEYOND | VIEW PDF OF CHARACTER SHEET

This character is Loriell Hawthorn, a ranger tiefling, also known as the character I've played the most, so far! The campaign I played this character in has been, unfortunately, discontinued due to the start of our DM's academic year. The party, of course, respected his decision, leading to the campaign being discontinued for two months now.

Despite playing this character for the longest time, I'll admit it: Loriell is the character I was the most dissatisfied with, roleplaying and stat wise. I dunno, perhaps it's because of my inexperience in playing as a ranger, perhaps it's because of my slight disconnect from the campaign, in general. (I'll admit it, again: I wasn't the best of friends with the guys I played with, save for one IRL friend.) However, I do not regret playing this character nor playing in this campaign whatsoever, and just like Calyspo up there, Loriell will forever be remembered in my heart.


MORRIA MELLOWS

VIEW ON DNDBEYOND | VIEW PDF OF CHARACTER SHEET

And here we have possibly my favorite character yet: Morria Mellows! They're a wonderful little sorcerer (stout) halfling with a heart for travel and a mind for languages! This is the character I'm currently playing in my current campaign, They Slash Them, with my current group of friendos, Rolling With The Boys! If ya wanna know more about our campaign (well, at the very least our campaign's first session), check out this blog over here!

I won't Ctrl+C - Ctrl+V my enthusiasm and excitement for this character and for this campaign, but I will say that I am absolutely loving the experience of playing this funny little accidental racist three-foot-tall being, and I'm looking forward to playing them more in the future!


That's all I've got for now! Stayed tuned for my next session, whenever and wherever that may be!

See ya'll! May out!
